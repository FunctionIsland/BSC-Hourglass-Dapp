1: Invest in the Game

To get started, you will need to buy some B1VS using BNB. When you invest, a 10% fee is incurred on your Deposit - this contributes to the Dividends earned by already-existing holders.

Dividends are paid to those who hold B1VS, so you'll want to Buy a decent amount to hold. The more B1VS you hold, the larger your "piece of the pie" and the more BNB Dividends you'll earn!


2: Refer Others to Play

Once you have some B1VS, you can use your Referral Link to bring others in to join the fun! You only need the one link - it's our site, with your wallet address at the end.

When others use your link, you get a bonus to your BNB Dividends! Just the same, they can also refer others with their link - so you should refer with your link fast, far and wide!


3: Compound your Earnings

Once you have a good HODL of B1VS, you'll want to start Compounding your earnings - that is, rolling them into more B1VS!

This will have the effect of growing your "slice of the pie", thus earning more Dividends, faster!


4: Sell B1VS & Withdraw BNB

Is it time to sell your B1VS? Are you sure? If so, sell them and receive BNB to withdraw to your wallet - then, you're out of the game!

Remember, you can join in again at any time to keep earning BNB dividends! Thanks for Playing!
