1: Invest in the Game

Buy some B1VS shares with BNB. When you do this, you'll incur a 10% Dividend Fee, paid out to existing B1VS holders. The more B1VS shares you hold, the larger your percentage of the dividends you are paid.


2: Refer Others to Play

Share your referral link around to friends, family and fellow degens - when they use it to buy some B1VS shares, you'll earn a bonus of 3% of their total deposit, as BNB dividends!


3: Compound your Earnings

Reinvesting your dividends is the fastest way to earn more in this game - doing this every day is almost akin to "compound interest". Are you a godly chad investor?


4: Sell B1VS & Withdraw BNB

When you're done earning dividends, you can sell your B1VS shares and withdraw the underlying collateralised BNB. Don't worry, you can join in again and start earning any time!
