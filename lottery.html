How to play P3Daily

◭ After 24 hours a winner is drawn and a new lottery round starts ◭ Each ticket costs 0.01 ETH ◭ Incoming ETH is used to feed the round pot and to buy P3D which is locked up ◭ The winner receives the jackpot which consists of the round pot and P3D dividends ◭ Be the one to validate the round after 24 hours to get a reward dependant on total jackpot size!
